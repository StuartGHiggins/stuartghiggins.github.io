Down to Earth

A radio mini-series for The Naked Scientists on space-technology spinoffs

I was commissioned by The Naked Scientists to produce a mini-series of episodes exploring different space-technology spin-offs, called Down to Earth. Each episode explores how a technology that was originally developed for space exploration became useful in life on the planet. Episodes were broadcast in the UK on BBC Radio and internationally as part of the main radio show.
