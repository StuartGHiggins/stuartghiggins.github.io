Dr Stuart G. Higgins

Physicist, bioengineer and
award-winning podcaster

I investigate the interface between cells, biomolecules and engineered materials.

Biology is complicated, simple methods aren't always enough to understand what's going on.

I use information-rich measurement analysis techniques to help uncover subtle effects in bioengineered materials and biosensors.

I love talking to other scientists, inspiring the next generation, and developing best practice in academia.
